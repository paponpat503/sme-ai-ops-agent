# Company Policy Knowledge Base

## Refund Policy

Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. Refunds for monthly plans are reviewed case by case when there is a verified platform failure.

Annual subscriptions are refundable within 14 days of purchase if the customer has not completed onboarding and has not used production workflow automation.

Enterprise refunds require manager approval and must include evidence from support tickets, billing records, and implementation notes.

## Escalation Policy

A customer should be escalated to a manager when any of the following is true:

- Enterprise customer has a critical support ticket open for more than 5 days.
- Payment is overdue and there is an unresolved product issue.
- A renewal account reports repeated workflow failure.
- The customer explicitly mentions churn, cancellation, or legal escalation.

## Customer Follow-Up Policy

High-risk customers should receive a follow-up within one business day. The message should acknowledge the issue, state the next action, and propose a support call.

Medium-risk customers should receive a standard follow-up email and a CRM task.

Low-risk customers should be monitored unless they have repeated issues.
